You feel the need to please your loved ones, friends, colleagues, and even strangers. You do not want to be a nuissance, but rather nice and easy to handle, so you agree to things that do not seem to be particularly important to you. You find it difficult to stand for yourself in both small and large matters. You let others control you because you want to avoid unpleasant consequences and situations.
